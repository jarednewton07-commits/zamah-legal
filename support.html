ZAMAH — Support

Need help with ZAMAH? We usually reply within 48 hours.

Contact us

Email jarednewton07@gmail.com with a description of the issue, your device/OS, and the app version (found in Settings → About). Screenshots help us resolve issues faster.

Common issues

IssueWhat to try
App won't load or is stuck loadingForce-close and reopen the app, or restart your device.
Session / course content won't loadCheck your internet connection, then try again. If it persists, try a different session or restart the app.
Subscription not showing as activeGo to Settings → Restore Purchases. If it still doesn't appear, email us with your purchase receipt.
Billing or refund questionPurchases are processed by Google Play/Apple — refund requests go through their support, but email us and we'll help however we can.
Data looks wrong or missingLearning data updates as you complete sessions and may take a moment to sync. If it's still off after a refresh, email us with details.
Account or data deletionUse Settings → Delete Account in the app, or see our Data & Account Deletion page.
